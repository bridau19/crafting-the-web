John Edward Daugherty
Born in Washington D.C.
Raised in Richmond, Virginia and Dazey, North Dakota
Bookish, Analytical, Patient


A picture of me, taken 4/28/1978 (6th grade).  I developed the film and printed the picture myself. Every Friday, there was a special class called “children’s choice”, and I chose to take photography class.  Back then, the class was less about taking pictures, but we used the darkroom to develop the film and make prints.

He had a Model T?

He had more than one! And a Model A as well. They had a lot of cars and old tractors (there were lots of small buildings and a big machine shed. Many of the buildings had a car inside. Often the upholstery was gone or mouse eaten, but a couple of the model T’s were restored. He used to drive them in parades).
