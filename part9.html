This is my mom.

My mom knows how to cook ramen, kimchi stew, galbi jjim, Korean side dishes, baked chicken, and five spice pork.
I think that makes up most of what I ate growing up. She definitely does not bake, however. "It's not traditional for Korean bears to bake." I learned baking from my dad.
